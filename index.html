About Us

In the beginning of 2020, we are unable to use canteen because of Covid-19. We cannot socialize, cannot share lecture notes and use school clubs efficently. So we think why we don't have an online platform that can provide us same benefits. So we begin this journey with this idea.

We aimed to provide students an online platform which is equitable, modernist and easy to use.

What we offer

  • Students can have struggle between about sharing lecture notes. We will design a fair platform

  • Students can socialize easily.

  • Only members of the university can join us. You cannot join if you are member of another university.
